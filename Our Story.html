Our Story

Established in 2009 by restaurateur Stefano Fabbri, Pomo has earned a reputation as an authentic Italian restaurant where diners can enjoy the truest flavors of Italy. Pomo’s four locations located in Scottsdale, Downtown Phoenix, Gilbert and Biltmore, are uniquely designed to it’s demographic to bring you an atmosphere that accommodates the Italian menu. Home made gelato is also available by Luna Gelateria, and a concept of Pomo Restaurant Group.
